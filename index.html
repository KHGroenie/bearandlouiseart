Meet the Artist

Painting is breathing
in colour

With a brush in hand and the South African landscape as her muse, Louise has spent a lifetime capturing moments of extraordinary beauty in oil and watercolour.

Her work is predominantly commission-based — painted with intimacy and intention for clients who want something lasting, something that belongs to them alone.

Her work hangs in homes across South Africa and is on permanent display at Gallery Blou Donkie in Clarens.

The Artist

HE GOES BY BEAR.
NOT MANY KNOW
HIS REAL NAME.

And perhaps that's the point. His art speaks loudly enough.

A graphic designer by trade with a pop art soul — Bear creates hyper-realistic portraits and caricatures, then takes it one step further: he cuts the board itself around the subject. No frame, no border, no rules. Just the face, floating free.

Seeing it in person is the only way to truly understand it.
