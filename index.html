Louise Hansen captures the light, land and life of South Africa in rich oils and delicate watercolours. Landscapes, florals, commissions — each painting unmistakably hers.

Meet the Artist

Painting is breathing
in colour

With a brush in hand and the South African landscape as her muse, Louise has spent a lifetime capturing moments of extraordinary beauty in oil and watercolour.

Her work is predominantly commission-based — painted with intimacy and intention for clients who want something lasting, something that belongs to them alone.

Her work hangs in homes across South Africa and is on permanent display at Gallery Blou Donkie in Clarens.

Learn to Paint

Art lessons with Louise

Louise occasionally offers painting lessons — whether you're visiting Clarens for the weekend or a local looking to develop your skills. Small groups or one-on-one, tailored to your level.

Workshop — Visiting Clarens?

Half-day or full-day painting workshops. Learn oil or watercolour technique in the landscape that inspires Louise's own work.

Ongoing Lessons — Locals Welcome

Regular lessons for beginners through to intermediate painters. Learn at your own pace in a relaxed, creative environment.

Book a Lesson via WhatsApp
Message Louise directly — she'll reply with available dates, what to bring, and what to expect. First-timers very welcome.
